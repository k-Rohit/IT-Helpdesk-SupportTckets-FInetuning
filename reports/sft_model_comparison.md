## Summary

The SFT-fine-tuned model consistently produces concise, professionally-toned, support-ticket-style responses that ask relevant clarifying questions before proposing a resolution, closely mirroring the structure and tone of real IT helpdesk tickets in the training data. The base model, in contrast, frequently drifts off-topic, hallucinates unrelated scenarios (e.g., fabricated hardware models, irrelevant meta-questions), gets stuck in repetitive loops, or gives generic textbook advice disconnected from an actual support workflow. This demonstrates the practical impact of instruction fine-tuning on a domain-adapted (CPT) base model.
